## How to Book

1. Visit the booking page or click "Book Now" on the website
2. Select service category (Color, Haircuts, or Waxing)
3. Choose specific service
4. Select preferred stylist (Virginia, Kim, or Alyssa)
5. Pick available date and time
6. Enter contact information
7. Confirm booking

## Booking Policies

**From Acuity settings:**
- Client phone number required for all appointments
- Clients can reschedule appointments
- Clients can cancel appointments
- SMS reminders enabled for all services

**Special Requirements:**
